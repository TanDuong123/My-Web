MANURE SPREADER SUPERFEX 700

The compact, smooth, efficient and DLG tested spreaders of the Superfex series with a wide operating range are characterized by optimal spreading of all agricultural goods.
The Superfex spreaders are available with both single-axle and tandem-axle, with either standard or universal spreading unit with comprehensive standard equipment and provide a unique value for money. With a large choice of options, the spreader can be equipped for individual needs and requirements.

HIGHLIGHTS

SPREADING UNIT

4 vertical beaters with bolt-on HARDOX sawteeth, spreading width up to 6 m, spreader rollers are driven individually by 4 mitre gears in an oil bath, removable spreading unit, hydraulic flap door
